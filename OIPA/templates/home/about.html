About

The OIPA framework has been developed by Zimmerman & Zimmerman.

Zimmerman & Zimmerman is a dutch design and consultancy firm that develops online concepts. Partnering with Akvo, they designed the framework and visual design Akvo open Aid that runs the codebase OIPA. The tools can be used by others to visualise other IATI data sets. Join us to create great apps and tools that make the most of open data!
